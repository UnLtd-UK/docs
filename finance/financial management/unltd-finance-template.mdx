## Overview

This finance template is designed to help social ventures plan and forecast their finances. It contains four working sheets: a completion checklist, sales assumptions, and cash flow forecasts for the current and next year.

## Sales Assumptions

Document how you are anticipating the next year's figures on the forecast, how you have come to your figures, pricing, and the status of any grants/loans (applied, not applied, approved).

### Income Assumptions

| Category | Example Assumption |
|----------|-------------------|
| Grants | £20k grant in August — not yet applied |
| Sales | Selling 20 items a month, increasing in 5s over the period; December increase due to Christmas; low period Aug–Oct |
| Contracts | CCG contract income coming in Oct — £10k contract over 3 years |

### Expenditure Assumptions

| Category | Example Assumption |
|----------|-------------------|
| Salaries | Additional staff taken on in Dec to help with Christmas sales |

### Summary Rows

- **Total Income** — sum of all income lines
- **Total Expenditure** — sum of all expenditure lines
- **Total Income - Expenditure** — monthly surplus/deficit
- **Balance brought forward** — closing balance from previous month
- **Closing balance** — running cash position

## Next Year Cash Flow
